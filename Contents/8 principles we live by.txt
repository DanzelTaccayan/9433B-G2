The 8 Principles that we believe by:
1. Animals should be taken care of, like human beings take care of each other.
2. Wild animals should be left alone nor should they be disturbed
3. Animals should be loved and they should not be killed nor be eaten.
4. Domestic animals should have a proper home to live in. 
5. Animals should not be abused.
6. People should treat animals with respect.
7. Be responsible in taking care of animals.
8. Always help an animal in need.
